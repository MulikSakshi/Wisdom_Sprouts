About 26 January

26 January is celebrated as Republic Day in India because on this day in 1950, the Constitution of India came into effect.
It marks the transition of India from a British Dominion to a sovereign republic.

Republic Day Facts

  • Republic Day is celebrated every year on 26 January.
  • The Constitution of India came into force in 1950.
  • A grand parade is held in New Delhi.
  • The President of India hoists the national flag.
